## HOW THIS RESEARCH WAS COLLECTED

**Methodology:** All data below was gathered via live web search on June 23, 2026 using the following process:
1. **Primary sources first:** Aspen Institute/Project Play surveys (cost), Opendorse annual NIL reports (NIL), Pew Research and platform statistics (social media)
2. **Cross-verification:** Each key figure was checked against 2–3 independent sources; discrepancies are flagged inline
3. **Source URLs included:** Every data point has a direct link to the page where it was found
4. **No extrapolation:** Where a year is missing from the primary series, that is noted explicitly. Estimated values derived from stated growth rates are clearly labeled as estimates

**Search queries used** (in order):
- "Aspen Institute Project Play youth sports cost per year historical data 2010 2015 2019 2020 2021 2022 2023 2024 annual family spending trend"
- "NIL market size by year 2021 2022 2023 2024 2025 2026 annual total value growth college athlete"
- "Instagram TikTok sports content growth statistics year by year 2015–2024 users accounts"
- "youth sports social media statistics trends 2018–2024 hashtag growth"
- "TikTok US users by year 2018–2025 million monthly active"
- "NIL year 2 2022-23 total market Opendorse $1.17 billion statistics"
- "youth sports market size revenue 2015 2016 2017 2018 US billion annual"
- "TikTok sports content statistics 2022 2023 2024 billion views engagement growth"
- Plus ~12 additional searches and ~10 WebFetch calls to primary source URLs
